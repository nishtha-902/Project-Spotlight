What We Do?

Project Spotlight is an online integrated platform designed to streamline the management and showcasing of student projects. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive ecosystem for students to collaborate, learn, and grow, taking their ideas from conception to fruition.

Track progress, set milestones, and receive feedback from mentors and peers. Access a vast library of resources, including tutorials, articles, and webinars.
